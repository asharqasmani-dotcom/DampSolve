Our Story

8 Years of Solving Hampshire's Damp Problems

Damp Solve was founded with one simple mission: to give Hampshire homeowners access to genuine, expert damp proofing advice without the sales tactics, inflated quotes or impersonal service that had become common in the industry.

We started small, serving our local area on the south coast, and grew through word of mouth and repeat customers — the only way a trade company worth its salt should grow. Over 8 years, we have carried out hundreds of damp surveys, treatment projects and timber remediation jobs across the whole of Hampshire.

"We have seen and tackled everything, from dry and wet rot to rising damp and mould. If damp is the problem, we can fix it."

What sets us apart is our approach: we diagnose the actual problem first, explain it clearly, give you honest options and then carry out the work to the highest standard — leaving your property clean and dry. No unnecessary work. No upselling. Just results.
