What We Do

Hampshire Damp Proofing & Timber Treatment Services

From a small damp patch to a full dry rot eradication — Damp Solve has the expertise to diagnose and solve every type of damp and timber problem in Hampshire properties.

Rising Damp

Ground moisture travels upward through masonry via capillary action, leaving tide marks, crumbling plaster and salt deposits on walls. Without a proper damp-proof course, it won't stop on its own.

Learn More →

Penetrating Damp

Water entering from outside through defective render, cracked pointing, faulty gutters or failed window seals. Typically shows as patches on walls or ceilings, often worse after rainfall.

Learn More →

Condensation & Mould

The most common damp problem in UK homes. Warm, moist air condenses on cold surfaces causing black mould growth, particularly in corners, behind furniture and around windows.

Learn More →

Dry Rot

A serious and fast-spreading fungal decay (Serpula lacrymans) that can travel through masonry as well as timber. Early identification and specialist treatment are essential to stop it spreading.

Learn More →

Wet Rot

Several fungal species cause wet rot in timber with high sustained moisture content. The affected wood becomes soft and spongy. Less aggressive than dry rot, but still needs prompt treatment.

Learn More →

Woodworm

Wood-boring beetle larvae tunnel through structural and decorative timber, leaving small round exit holes. An active infestation can weaken joists, floorboards and roof timbers if untreated.

Learn More →

Damp Surveys

A thorough property inspection with moisture meter readings and a written report detailing findings and recommendations. Essential for home buyers, landlords and anyone with unexplained damp problems.

Learn More →
