What We Do

Our Damp Proofing Services

From the first damp patch to full timber treatment — Damp Solve handles every kind of moisture problem in Hampshire homes and properties.

Rising Damp

Ground moisture travelling upward through walls, causing tide marks, flaking plaster and that tell-tale damp smell at skirting level.

Learn More →

Penetrating Damp

Water entering from outside through cracked render, faulty pointing or failed window seals — damp patches that appear after heavy rain.

Learn More →

Condensation & Mould

The most common form of household damp — black mould on walls, streaming windows and a musty smell, caused by poor ventilation.

Learn More →

Dry Rot

A serious fungal decay that spreads through timber and masonry — recognisable by brittle, cubed cracking and a distinctive mushroom smell.

Learn More →

Wet Rot

Timber decay caused by sustained high moisture — soft, spongy wood with darkened areas, often hidden behind paint or floor coverings.

Learn More →

Woodworm

Small round exit holes in timber and fine sawdust (frass) are the telltale signs of wood-boring beetle larvae damaging your timbers.

Learn More →
